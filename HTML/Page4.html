Learning Objective 3: Grasp the benefits of boxing

Under this learning objective, you will be able to know:

  1. How boxing could create physical wellness:
    • Aids in weight loss and fat burn
    • Increase in muscle strength
    • Maintain cardioascular health
    • Enhance flexibility, body coordination and balance
  2. What mental benefits boxing brings:
    • Improved confidence & self-esteem
    • Relieved stress
    • Learn about yourself
  3. Make friends who have the same interest and goal!
